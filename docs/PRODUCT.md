# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Restaurant staff, specifically chefs and kitchen managers, operating in a fast-paced, high-pressure, loud, and visually busy kitchen environment. They view the interface on large mounted touchscreen displays or tablets.

## Product Purpose
foodRMS Kitchen Display System (KDS) exists to orchestrate order preparation with zero friction. It turns chaos into clarity, ensuring orders are prepared correctly, on time, and delivered to customers flawlessly.

## Brand Personality
Energetic, highly visible, utilitarian, unignorable.

## Anti-references
- Boring enterprise gray SaaS software.
- Overly subtle consumer apps (no low-contrast grays on whites).
- Corporate material design.

## Design Principles
- **Unignorable Clarity**: Information hierarchy must survive being read from 6 feet away.
- **Zero Ambiguity**: State changes must be absolute and obvious.
- **High Energy Utility**: The design should match the intense energy of a commercial kitchen.
- **Immediate Affordance**: Buttons must feel tactile and unmissable.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- High contrast for visibility under bright kitchen lights.
- Large tap targets for users operating touchscreens quickly or with gloves.
