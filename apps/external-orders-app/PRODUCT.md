# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Delivery employees and restaurant staff taking photos of physical receipts to digitize delivery orders. They need a fast, error-free, low-friction scanning process.

## Product Purpose
To capture physical receipts using the device camera, send them to an OCR engine, and convert them into digital orders in the FoodRMS system. Success is measured by speed of capture and clarity of the scanned image.

## Brand Personality
Professional, fast, confident, rugged, and highly efficient. The interface should feel like a high-end industrial tool, not a playful consumer app.

## Anti-references
Generic web forms, basic unstyled camera views, anything that feels slow or clunky.

## Design Principles
- **Clarity over cleverness:** Guide the user to center the receipt.
- **Immediate feedback:** Haptic or visual feedback on capture.
- **Robustness:** Handles low-light or blurry conditions gracefully by prompting the user.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
High contrast text on camera overlays. Large, easy-to-tap capture buttons.
