# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Restaurant owners, managers, and frontline staff (cashiers, baristas). The system serves both administrative management and active operational tasks.

## Product Purpose
A comprehensive Restaurant SaaS Dashboard for managing multiple branches, staff performance, menu engineering, and real-time order processing.

## Brand Personality
Bold, Energetic, Functional. The "Neo-Brutalist" aesthetic uses high-contrast colors, thick black borders, and heavy shadows to create a design that feels alive, modern, and highly accessible.

## Anti-references
Generic corporate SaaS, soft/muted pastel dashboards, "safe" minimalist interfaces with thin lines and subtle grays.

## Design Principles
1. **Bold Clarity**: Use thick borders and high contrast to make every element distinct.
2. **Functional Playfulness**: Emojis and vibrant colors serve as functional cues, not just decoration.
3. **Operational Speed**: POS and order management must prioritize speed and physical tappability.
4. **Data Vitality**: Charts and metrics should feel "live" and urgent.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- High contrast by default (Neo-Brutalism).
- Support for RTL (Arabic) layout.
- Large hit targets for touch interfaces (POS).
